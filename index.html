Presentation

A presentation is a formal talk given to an audience, usually about the particular topic that the speaker has conducted. It is typically to demonstrate, introduce, lecture, or speech meant to inform, persuade, or build good quality. The goal of a presentation is to share new information with others in the field and to receive feedback on the work.

Poster Presentation

A poster presentation is a formal and research-based demonstration of your work. Through text, charts, graphs, and other visual aids, a poster presentation provides a visual representation of your research. A poster presentation enables the author to meet and discuss things informally with interested viewers, thereby promoting a greater exchange of ideas and networking opportunities. On this National Mathematics Day, let's take advantage of this opportunity, which allows viewers to read your research at their own pace and interact with you by asking questions about your methods and findings.
